VISUAL BEATMAP EDITOR

SPAWN NOTES

Notes spawn at the blue playhead. Drag them to adjust timing.

HOW TO USE

  • Play/Pause: Listen to the track.
  • Seek: Click the timeline background to move the blue playhead.
  • Spawn: Click a button above to spawn a note at the playhead.
  • Drag: Move notes left/right (snaps to grid).
  • Delete: Right-click a note to remove it.
  • Rules: Notes cannot overlap in the same lane!
